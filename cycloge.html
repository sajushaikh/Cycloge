Powers

Reflection of token holder :

As a Cycloge token holder, you can enjoy daily Doge coin reflections based on your holdings! That's right, every 24 hours you'll receive reflections straight to your wallet.

Construction DAPP :

Get ready to unleash your creativity with Cycloge's Construction DApp! This innovative platform will allow artists to design and showcase their very own Cycloge NFTs. Top Artists will be selecting through community voting weekly and they will get a chance to list their NFTs on the marketplace and even set their own bidding amounts for trade.

Reflection of NFT holder :

Holder of our incredible NFT will recieve even higher reflections percentages compared to token holders! This means even more Doge Coin reflections for you. Be sure to check out our Tokenomics for all the details.
